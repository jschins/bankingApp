Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-29 · deoudegracht.nl

This application ("bankingApp") is a personal, non-commercial tool used by an individual (the "operator") to retrieve bank account information via the Enable Banking / PSD2 Open Banking API, on behalf of the operator and of a limited number of consenting individuals (the "account holders") who are typically family members.

What data is accessed

With each account holder's explicit consent, the application accesses account details, balances and transaction data for accounts that the relevant account holder personally links. Each account holder authorizes access only to their own accounts, through their own bank authentication. No data is accessed for any person who has not personally linked their accounts.

Written consent

Every account holder whose banking transactions are processed provides written consent to the operator processing that data for the purposes described in this policy, in addition to the consent given through their bank's authentication. This written consent may be withdrawn at any time.

How data is used and stored

Data is retrieved on demand and stored only locally on the operator's own computer for personal bookkeeping on behalf of the consenting account holders. The data is not shared with, sold to, or transmitted to any third party. Credentials and cryptographic keys are kept by the operator and are only distributed within self-contained software provided directly to the consenting account holders themselves; they are never shared with any third party.

Consent and revocation

Access requires each account holder's explicit consent given through their bank's authentication. Consent is time-limited and can be revoked at any time via the bank, by withdrawing written consent to the operator, or by deleting the application's access. On revocation, processing of that account holder's data stops and their locally stored data is deleted on request.
